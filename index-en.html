About Cohen Hummus

Cohen Hummus is a special place where we prepare dishes with love and dedication. Our restaurant specializes in authentic Middle Eastern dishes, focusing on high-quality ingredients and real homemade taste.

We are proud to offer you a unique culinary experience with the tastiest dishes, from fresh hummus and warm crispy pita to special dishes developed in our kitchen. Chef Rotem Cohen crafts each dish like a work of art, with true love for food.
